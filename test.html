MUSTANG RECIPE

This is the procudure on how to make a great cup of coffee

You will need the following components;

  1. 500ml pureboiling water
  2. one tea spoonful of soluble coffee
  3. A half teaspoolful of ginger
  4. Two teaspoonful of sugar
  5. A cup
  6. A snack

PROCEDURE

  1. Take a clean mug
  2. Add a half teaspoolful of ginger in the mug
  3. Add two to three teaspoolful of sugar to the content
  4. Add a one tea spoonful of soluble coffee
  5. Add boiling water to almost cup full
  6. Stir until all the components dissolve
  7. Add lemonade but its optional
  8. Take your snack and enjoy your cup of coffee
  9. Put on some music for the mood too!
